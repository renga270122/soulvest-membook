# SoulVest LoveBook Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

SoulVest LoveBook values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## What We Collect
- **Personal Information:** Name, email, and any information you enter in your memory book.
- **Usage Data:** Analytics on signups, logins, and app usage (anonymized).

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide and improve the app experience.
- To personalize your memory book and features.
- To analyze usage for product improvement.

## Data Security
- Your data is stored securely and never sold or shared with third parties.
- Guest mode data is not saved to our servers.

## Your Choices
- You can delete your account and data at any time by contacting support.
- You may use the app as a guest for maximum privacy.
